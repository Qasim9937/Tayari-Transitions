Vision

The .transitions co-Builders Fellowship envisions a continent where talent is activated and systems re-imagined through co-building approaches, shared ownership, local agency and regenerative leadership

Across Africa and beyond, some of the most catalytic ventures have not emerged from lone innovators or traditional top-down institutions, but from the collaborative ambition of intrapreneurs, mission-driven individuals embedded within systems who mobilize their expertise, time, networks, and influence to co-create solutions that are both impactful and commercially viable. These intrapreneurs do more than build programs; they build systems, often in informal or under-resourced environments, by leveraging shared values, mutual accountability, and a spirit of co-ownership.

The .transitions co-Builders Fellowship exists to make Africa ready for what’s next by connecting doers who are ready to shape what’s possible.

Mission

A purpose-driven and impact-oriented mission to embed Africa’s #NextGen systems shaping co-builders at the forefront of solving complex challenges of our time

the .transitions co-Builders Fellowship is founded upon four key tenets we believe are necessary to support the dynamic nature of demographic transitions, talent incubation, enterprise development employability, innovation and digital transformation.

Ingenuity

The quality of being resourceful lies in making tools and intelligence accessible. We believe that fostering diverse, cross-industry collaboration sparks creative solutions and new perspectives, which are imperative for driving global growth.

Intelligence

"A rising tide lifts all boats." Cultivating an esteemed, mission-driven community of global leaders, united by a shared vision for transformative impact, is crucial for uniting the best ideas, which in turn lead to impactful outcomes.

Wisdom

To become wise is to harness appropriate resources and acquire the knowledge to contribute to what matters most. At our core, we believe that innovation management is a disciplined and strategic approach essential for driving business success and creating a global impact.

Patience

Patience empowers long-term vision. Embracing the time it takes to build impactful outcomes allows for reflection, iteration, and growth — foundations of sustainable success and meaningful innovation.
